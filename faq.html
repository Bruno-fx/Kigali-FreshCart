Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Information

What is Kigali Shopping Mall?

Kigali Shopping Mall is an online marketplace that connects you with a wide range of products across different categories. Our platform aims to make shopping easy, convenient, and secure.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via our Contact Us page for any assistance or queries you may have.

Are all products sold directly by Kigali Shopping Mall?

No, Kigali Shopping Mall serves as a platform for various vendors. We carefully select vendors to ensure quality, but each vendor manages their own product listings and sales.

Orders and Shipping

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our categories or use the search bar to find your desired product. Once found, click "Add to Cart" and proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.

What are the shipping options and delivery times?

We offer standard and express shipping options. Standard shipping typically takes 3-7 business days, while express shipping takes 1-3 business days, depending on your location. Exact details are provided during checkout.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, a tracking number will be provided to you via email, allowing you to monitor your order status on our website.

Returns and Refunds

What is the return policy?

Our return policy allows returns within 14 days of receiving your order. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Visit our Returns Policy page for more details.

How can I request a refund?

To request a refund, please contact our customer service team with your order details. Refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 7-10 business days upon approval.

Can I exchange an item?

Yes, exchanges are available for eligible products. Please contact us within 14 days of receiving your item to arrange an exchange. More information is available on our Returns Policy page.

Payment and Security

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express, as well as mobile payment options like PayPal and Google Pay.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security. All transactions are encrypted, and we comply with industry standards to protect your payment data. For details, visit our Privacy Policy.

Will I receive an invoice for my purchase?

Yes, after completing your purchase, a detailed invoice will be sent to your registered phone number as SMS.
